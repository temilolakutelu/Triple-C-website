Founded in 2009 by Pearl C.A.Ernest et al

We Are A Philanthropically based Foundation with the Aim of Eradicating Poverty in as much lives as possible.

Our long term investment in empowering African entrepreneurs is symbolic of our philosophy of Africa's capitalism which positions its private sector and most importantly entrepreneurs as the catalyst for the social and economical development of the continent.

Our Programme is built on a unique system of effective and intensive support to African entrepreneurs.

We are here to turn dreams into reality for Entrepreneurs...
